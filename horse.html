My Love for Horses

Ever since I was little, I've adored horses. But, I've never been able to own one, or afford riding lessons. But, you can always dream, right?

From having a collection of horse plushies, books, models, full sketchbooks and an imaginary herd, horses were the focus in my young life. I remember the first time I went horseback riding on the beach with my family, and my horse took off at a canter(gallop? lol, I dunno). It felt like flying.
